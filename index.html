Projects

Knee Cartilage and Bone Segmentation

Developed a pipeline for image reconstruction and automatic segmentation of MRI images of the knee, avoiding costly manual segmentation by radiologists. This method finished in 4th place out of 87 competing teams at the 2022 MICCAI K2S Challenge.

Technologies: Python, PyTorch, SLURM

Multi-modal imaging super-resolution for Sodium MRI

Fuses a low-resolution image with higher-resolution images from various techniques to enhance the resolution of the original image. The method reduces inference time from ~616s using PLS method to ~1.2s using a dual-encoder Y-net architecture.

Technologies: Python, PyTorch, SLURM

Advanced Ultrasound Signal Processing for Equipment inspections (AUSPEX)

Developed software and inspection tools for Petróleo Brasileiro S.A., creating methods to increase the reliability of submarine inspections and reduce inspection costs. Reduced specimen profile estimation error by an average of 5.6x.
